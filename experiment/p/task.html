Ala! They found some magic eggs, and they are curious to find out what magic power these eggs have.

What we know about magic eggs:

  • If you let a magic egg touch upon some stuff, the magic egg may cause the properties of the stuff it touched upon to change.
  • Properties of the magic egg indicate what magic power it has.
  • Magic eggs laid by the same hen share the same magic power.

In this task, you will first check Alice’s findings, and then Bob’s.
